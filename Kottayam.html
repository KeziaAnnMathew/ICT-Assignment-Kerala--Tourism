City of Letters

'Kottayam' literally means the interior of a fort—Kotta + Akam
Most of India's natural rubber originates from the acres of well-kept plantations
of Kottayam. Due to its its contribution to print media and literature,
it is called as “Akshara Nagari” (“city of letters”).

Area: 2,208 km²

Population: 19.75 Lakhs
